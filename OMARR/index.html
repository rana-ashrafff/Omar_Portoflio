About Me

Hi, I’m Omar Zidan — a passionate Video Editor, Motion Graphics Artist, and aspiring Game Developer with over a year of professional experience creating content that not only looks visually stunning but also tells compelling stories. I specialize in Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects, crafting everything from short-form social media content to marketing campaigns and educational videos. I also have experience in photography, bringing a keen eye for composition, lighting, and visual storytelling to every project. In addition, I build games using Unity and have solid programming skills in Python and C#, allowing me to combine creative design with technical implementation. My journey in video editing and digital content creation began over five years ago as a hobby and quickly developed into a true passion. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in detail, color, pacing, and storytelling, enabling me to transform ideas into visuals that genuinely resonate. Currently, I’m pursuing a Computer Engineering degree at the Arab Academy for Science, Technology & Maritime Transport, which provides me with a strong technical foundation. This combination of engineering knowledge, creative vision, and programming expertise allows me to approach projects with both analytical problem-solving and artistic innovation. Outside of editing and development, I enjoy exploring the latest trends in design, storytelling, and digital media. My goal is to continuously grow, experiment, and ultimately lead projects that not only engage audiences but also make a meaningful impact.
